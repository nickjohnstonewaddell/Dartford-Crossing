You can check the charge for a particular vehicle by entering its number plate.

No charge

There is no charge if you:

  • drive a motorcycle, moped or quad bike
  • use the crossing at night, between 10pm and 6am
  • get free UK vehicle tax because of a disability (your vehicle will be checked automatically when you cross - you do not need to do anything)

Local resident discount scheme

If you pay council tax to Dartford Borough Council or Thurrock Council and are part of the local resident discount scheme, the annual cost is either:

  • £20.00 for as many crossings as you want
  • £10.00 for up to 50 crossings (you can then pay another £10 for as many further crossings as you want)

Find your local council.

Cars, motorhomes and minibuses

This includes cars with trailers. It only includes minibuses that have 9 or fewer seats (including the driver’s seat).

The cost is:

  • £2.50 as a one-off payment
  • £2.00 if you have a pre-pay account

Buses, coaches, vans and other goods vehicles

If the vehicle has 2 axles, the cost is:

  • £3.00 as a one-off payment
  • £2.63 if you have a pre-pay account

If the vehicle has more than 2 axles, the cost is:

  • £6.00 as a one-off payment
  • £5.19 if you have a pre-pay account

Fines

You will get a fine if you do not pay the charge. You must pay it within 28 days. The fine is:

  • £35 if paid within 14 days
  • £70 if paid within 28 days

You'll need to pay the crossing charge as well as the fine.

You can pay or challenge the fine online or call the Dart Charge contact centre.
