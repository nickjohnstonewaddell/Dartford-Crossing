Find out about charges and fines, including crossings for which there is no charge. How much you pay depends on the type of vehicle and if you already have an account.

You can also use this service to:

  • pay or challenge a penalty charge notice (PCN)
  • pay for future crossings automatically by creating an account
  • apply for a local resident discount
  • check your crossing and payment history

You must pay the Dart Charge by midnight the day after you cross.

You need to pay for every time you use the crossing, including weekends and bank holidays. Non-UK registered vehicles must also pay the charge.

Other ways to pay

You can:

  • pay with cash at your nearest Payzone store
  • set up an account by post
  • pay in advance or top-up your account by post
  • pay or set up an account by phone
