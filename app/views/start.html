Start now

As well as paying for a crossing, this allows you to:

  • pay or challenge a penalty charge notice (PCN)
  • pay for future crossings automatically by creating an account
  • apply for a local resident discount
  • check your crossing and payment history

Other ways to pay

The online service accessed above is the quickest way to pay. There are also other options, however. You can:

  • pay with cash at your nearest Payzone store
  • set up an account by post
  • pay in advance or top-up your account by post
  • pay or set up an account by calling the Dart Charge contact centre

You can get in touch every day, from 8am to 8pm. Find out about call charges.
